About Elderly Care & Mindful Support Hub

Empowering Nepal's elderly community with compassion, dignity, and support.

Our Mission

To provide quality care, resources, and a vibrant community for elderly individuals in Nepal, ensuring they live with dignity and joy.

Our Vision

To create a society where all seniors are respected, empowered, and able to lead fulfilling lives.

Meet Our Leadership

Arjun Thapa
Founder & Director

Our Journey

  • 2018: Elderly Care & Mindful Support Hub founded in Kathmandu.
  • 2019: Launched first wellness and caregiver training programs.
  • 2021: Expanded to serve over 1,000 seniors across Nepal.
  • 2023: Introduced digital resource center and online community events.
