About Us

Elderly Care & Mindful Support Hub is dedicated to supporting the wellbeing of elderly individuals and their families in Nepal.

Our platform connects families, caregivers, and elderly users, providing job opportunities, secure communication, wellness resources, and mental health support.

We believe in dignity, compassion, and holistic care for our elders. Our team is based in Pokhara, Nepal, and is committed to making a positive difference in our community.

Key Features:

  • Role-based job board for caregivers and families
  • Secure chat and messaging system
  • Wellness articles and educational videos
  • Mental health support and resources
