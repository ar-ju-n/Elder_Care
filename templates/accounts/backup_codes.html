What are backup codes?

Backup codes are one-time use codes that can be used to access your account if you lose access to your authenticator app. Each code can only be used once.

How to use backup codes
  1. When signing in, after entering your password, click "Having trouble with your authenticator app?"
  2. Select "Use a backup code"
  3. Enter one of your unused backup codes
